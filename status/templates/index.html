Genome Assembly Production Tracking Tool

This site is under active development by the CNAG team (namely, Tyler Alioto). Currently, it is merely a dev site for testing ideas with respect to assembly production for the BGE ERGA stream and/or the Catalan Initiative for the Earth BioGenome Project (CBP). If you'd like to browse the api or fiddle with entering test data, please register an account via the link at the top right and we will manually activate your account and grant limited permissions. If you'd like to help develop, head on over to https://github.com/cnag-aat/ERGA-Status

DISCLAIMER: Things are likely to change and the site may occasionally be down. This is NOT a production site (yet;-)

BGE T9.3 Assembly Delivery M4-38 CNAG-CRG (lead),Sanger,IZW,Genoscope,UU

This Task will coordinate the use of the assembly workflows by in-Project partners and the genomics community to process the different datatypes produced by the sequencing centres and deliver high-quality genome assemblies. It will also provide support and guidance to maintain common quality control standards and timely delivery of the outputs.

Reference Genome Assembly Quality Controls
